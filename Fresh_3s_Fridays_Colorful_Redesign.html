No Waiting. No Cutoffs.
Your Child Starts When They're Ready.

Most preschools operate on a fixed calendar. If your child's birthday falls after the enrollment cutoff, you wait — sometimes an entire year — before they can begin.

At Spark, we do it differently.

Your child is eligible for Fresh 3's Fridays starting on the day they turn three. Mid-October birthday? They can join in October. January birthday? January it is. There is no "you'll have to wait until next fall."

This matters because three-year-olds are ready to learn when they are ready — not when the calendar says so. The research on early childhood brain development is clear: the preschool years are the most critical window for building the neural pathways children will rely on for language, math, social skills, and problem-solving for the rest of their lives. Every month counts.

Fresh 3's Fridays exists so that no child has to wait.

Are There Any Age Requirements?

Your child must be three years old by their first day and must be fully potty trained before they begin. That is it. No testing. No evaluation. Just a family ready to take that first step.

Questions About Fresh 3's Fridays

Does my child need to be potty trained?
Yes. All students at Spark Academy — across every program — must be fully potty trained before their first day. If your child is still working on potty training, we recommend waiting until they have achieved independence before enrolling.
Can my child enroll if they have never been to preschool before?
Absolutely. This program is designed specifically as a first school experience for families who want to take that first step gently. Many of our Friday families have children who have never been in a classroom setting before.
What if my child's birthday is mid-year — can they really start then?
Yes. Unlike most preschools that hold a fixed enrollment cutoff at the start of the school year, Spark welcomes Fresh 3's Friday students starting on their third birthday, whenever that falls during the school year. Call us at 309-291-3292 and we will get you set up.
Is Fresh 3's Fridays the same quality as the full 3's program?
It is the same program. Same teachers. Same Purposeful Play Framework. Same individualized approach. Same enrichment, Developmental Playroom, and daily schedule structure. The only variable is the number of days per week.
What happens if we want to add more days later?
Friday students can join the Monday/Wednesday 3's Preschool at any point during the school year. Just let us know when you are ready and we will walk you through the transition.
Is there a waitlist?
Fresh 3's Fridays is capped at 12 students. If you are interested, we recommend scheduling a tour soon to check on availability.
