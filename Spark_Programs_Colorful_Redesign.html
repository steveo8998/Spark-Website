The Same Foundation. Every Day.
Across Every Program.

Regardless of which program your child is in, every day at Spark Academy includes the same core structure:

The Purposeful Play Framework
Dr. Peterson's doctoral-research-informed curriculum approach, built on the Experience Early Learning whole-child model. Every activity is designed with developmental intent.
The Developmental Playroom
A dedicated, monthly-rotating play environment unlike anything else in the area. Student-driven, theme-aligned, and changed out completely every month.
Five Daily Enrichments
Spanish on Monday, STEM on Tuesday, Art on Wednesday, Communication on Thursday, Music and Movement on Friday. Not extras -- core programming.
A 5:1 Student-to-Teacher Ratio
Maximum 10-12 students per class. The lowest ratio of any preschool program in the area.
Credentialed Teachers
Every Spark teacher holds a degree and credential in education.
All-Inclusive Tuition
No supply lists. No hidden fees. No additional costs. Everything is included -- teachers, curriculum, supplies, art materials, and daily snacks.

A child can begin with Fresh 3's Fridays the day they turn three. They can grow into the full 3's program. They can deepen their experience through the 4's and Kindergarten Prep years. And they can complete their early childhood education in Spark's private Kindergarten -- surrounded by teachers they know, in a community that has been part of their family's life for years.

Not every child follows every step. Many families enter at 4 or at Kindergarten Prep. All are warmly welcomed wherever they begin. But for families who find their way to Spark early, the continuity of relationships, expectations, and environment across multiple years is something no single-year program can replicate.
