What to Expect on Your Tour

When you visit Spark Academy, here's what your tour will look like:

Meet Dr. Peterson. Every tour begins with a personal conversation with our founder, Dr. Michelle Peterson, Ed.D. She'll ask about your child, your family's priorities, and what you're looking for in a preschool.
See the classrooms. You'll walk through our learning spaces — including the Developmental Playroom, which changes monthly with fresh materials designed to build creativity, problem-solving, and collaboration.
Watch learning in action. If your visit aligns with the school day, you'll see our teachers and children in the middle of a real morning — not a rehearsed presentation.
Ask anything. There's no script and no sales pitch. Bring every question you have — about curriculum, daily schedules, how teachers handle big emotions, or how Spark supports children with different learning styles. Your tour is a conversation, not a performance.
